If you don’t think an urgent treatment centre is right for you

What you’ve told us suggests you need to go to an urgent treatment centre to get help.

Ask to see a mental health trained nurse if they have one. Or ask to be referred to a local psychiatry service when you’re seen by the nurse or doctor.

Find an urgent mental health helpline near you

If you haven’t hurt yourself and you’d rather speak to someone instead, NHS urgent mental health helplines are open 24 hours a day. Deaf people can use text relay.
